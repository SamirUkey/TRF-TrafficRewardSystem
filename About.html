About Us

TrafficRewards is on a mission to make the roads safer by incentivizing good driving behavior. While traffic management systems worldwide impose penalties on violators, there are no corresponding incentives for those obeying the traffic rules. It is this majority population of rule-abiding individuals that TrafficRewards aims to empower through positive reinforcement.

By participating in the initiative, individuals not only earn rewards but also become part of a larger network of community striving for the common goal of making India road accident free. Let's join our hands together, for it is only with collective efforts, that we can create safer roads and a promising future for all.
